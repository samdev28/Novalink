Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on NovaLink?

‚ñº

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Get Started" button on the homepage, fill in your email and password, and you're ready to go. You can also sign up using your Google or GitHub account for even faster registration.

Is NovaLink free to use?

‚ñº

Yes! NovaLink offers a free plan with all core features including Idea Hub, Project Boards, Showcase Space, and Community Threads. We also offer premium plans with advanced features and increased storage for teams who need more.

How do I invite team members to a project?

‚ñº

Once you've created a project, go to the Project Settings and click "Invite Members". Enter the email addresses of the people you want to add, and they'll receive an invitation to join your project.

Can I change my username?

‚ñº

Yes! You can change your username anytime in your Account Settings. Simply go to Settings ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí Username and update it. Your old username will be available for others to use.

How do I delete my account?

‚ñº

To delete your account, go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your projects and data will be deleted.

Is my data secure on NovaLink?

‚ñº

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is encrypted in transit and at rest. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
